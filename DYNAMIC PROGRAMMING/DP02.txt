1D DYNAMIC PRORAMMING
IDENTIFICATION: Count the total number of ways / min or max output of the many ways possible to do something
    1. Try all possible ways
    2. Count possible ways
    3. Find out min/max of all possible ways based on some parameter

SOLUTION:
    1. Represent problem in terms of indexes
    2. Do all possible stuff on the index according to problem statement
    3. If question says
        a. count: sum up all stuff
        b. min: find min of all stuff
        c. max: find max of all stuff
